TOEFL Overview

TOEFL - Test of English as a Foreign Language is a standardized English proficiency test that assesses English Language skills in the areas of Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking. Candidates desirous of pursuing further studies at undergraduate or graduate levels are required to take TOEFL to demonstrate their English proficiency. This test is offered 30-40 times a year, and is accepted by all Institutions in the USA as well as by some Institutions in UK and Canada.

Types Of TOEFL Exam

TOEFL Internet-Based Test (or TOEFL iBT)

  • Can be taken at a TOEFL center or at home.
  • The test is offered more than 170 times a year.
  • The at-home version of the test is available 24 hours a day, 4 days a week.
  • It is accepted worldwide by  11,500+ universities in  160+ countries.

TOEFL Essentials

  • It takes about 90 minutes to complete the test.
  • The reading and listening passages on the Essentials Test are shorter and revolve around English in daily life.
  • The test is accepted in over 500 institutions worldwide.

TOEFL Scoring
  • Each of the four sections of the test is scored on a scale of 0 to 30 points.
  • You will also get an overall score of 0-120 based on the individual scores.
  • For every right answer, you score a point and there is no negative marking for wrong answers.
  • Your TOEFL score is valid for two years from the date of publication of the result.
  • You are allowed to send your score up to 4 universities free of charge.

FAQs

  • What is the difference between IELTS / PTE / TOEFL / DUOLINGO ?

    IELTS is a paper based exam and PTE, TOEFL & DUOLINGO are online exams. All of them are English proficiency tests

  • How many hours of coaching is given for IELTS / PTE / TOEFL / DUOLINGO?

    For IELTS / PTE / TOEFL / DUOLINGO, 30 hours of training is given. Students will also get the opportunity to attend TOEFL practice test conducted by Edwise.

  • What modules do I have to study as part of my TOEFL exam preparation?

    The modules are based on the sections in the TOEFL exams which are Reading, Writing, Listening and Speaking.

  • Does Edwise assist with registration and payments for the exams?

    For TOEFL, Candidates can pay only by an international credit card. We provide TOEFL coaching and facilitate the registration for the exam.

  • How to prepare for TOEFL?

    Edwise’s TOEFL preparation coaching services will make you familiar with the test format, practice each section regularly by using TOEFL prep books, take practice tests, and improve your English language skills.

  • What is the cost of score reporting for TOEFL ?

    TOEFL: Free score reporting for 4 universities up to 2 days before the examination. Additional score report costs Rs. 1,950 per university.

  • Is basic English grammar included in the TOEFL exam coaching?

    Basic English grammar will not be included in the TOEFL classes provided by Edwise.
