Top 10 Universities and Colleges in Canada for Hospitality

Education Abroad in Canada is known to be one of the most rewarding ventures that students can choose in their careers. Study in Canada is cost effective, premium grade and hosts the most diverse cultures on campus. Canada is at the core of innovation in various fields of study and education. New methods of teaching and research have made Canada one of the top countries to study in. Canada boasts an amazing environment in which students from different ethnicities can interact with each other and in turn build a community. Courses in Canada are famous among Indian students who wish to study abroad. Here are the durations for different types of programs:

Bachelor's Degree:

A bachelor's degree in hospitality management is typically a four-year undergraduate program in Canada.

Diploma:

Diploma in hospitality management In Canada are generally shorter in duration compared to bachelor's degrees. They typically last for two to three years.

Certificate Programs:

Certificate programs can range from a few months to a year in duration.

Are there any language requirements for international students interested in studying hospitality in Canada?

Yes, there are langauge requirements. English and French being their national languages are prefered. The most widely accepted English proficiency tests in Canada are:

  • International English Language Testing System (IELTS)

  • Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL)

  • Canadian English Language Proficiency Index Program (CELPIP)

    • Each institution may have its specific language requirements, and the minimum score required for admission may vary according to the program and the level of study.
