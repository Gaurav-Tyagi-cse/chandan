Know It All About Pursuing Tourism and Hospitality in Dubai

Owing to its first-rate educational institutions, central location, and multicultural setting, Dubai is a popular study abroad destination that provides students with an amalgamation of academic success, cultural diversity, and employment prospects in a rapidly advancing economy.

Integrating theoretical learning with real-world experience in one of the major centres for travel and hospitality worldwide, Tourism and Hospitality programs in Dubai generally emphasize on offering thorough knowledge of the industry. These programs typically range from short courses lasting a few months to bachelor's degrees spanning 3-4 years, with some universities also offering master's programs lasting 1-2 years.

Why study Tourism and Hospitality in Dubai?

Studying Tourism and Hospitality in Dubai is a wise choice for the reasons mentioned below:

  • Academic Excellence: Dubai is home to prestigious universities that offer specialized tourism and hospitality courses.
  • Secure and Contemporary City: The country is renowned for its cleanliness, safety, and contemporary facilities.
  • Cultural Variation: Students who study in Dubai gain exposure to a multicultural setting with more than 200 nationalities.
  • Top-Tier Infrastructure: Cutting-edge resorts, hotels, and entertainment centres.
  • Central Location: Gateway between East and West, promoting worldwide connectivity. 
  • Vibrant Sector: One of the rapidly expanding tourism and hospitality industries in the world.
  • Innovative Methods: Groundbreaking advancements in luxury, innovation, and service standards.
  • Government Assistance: The government places a strong emphasis on promoting tourism and hospitality.
  • Possibilities for Networking: Access to industry specialists and events.
  • Employment Opportunities: There is an increasing demand for qualified professionals in the local and regional markets.

What’s the cost of pursuing Tourism and Hospitality in Dubai?

Based on the institution and degree of study, the cost of pursuing a Tourism and Hospitality degree in Dubai differs. The tuition fees for undergraduate programs usually cost between AED 40,000-AED 80,000 per year. On the contrary, postgraduate programs usually cost between AED 50,000-AED 120,000 in tuition annually. Moreover, costs of living, which in Dubai can be high, estimated at AED 3,000-AED 5,000 every month for food, housing, transit, and other individual expenditures, should be taken into account by students.

To assist in alleviating these expenses, scholarships and funding alternatives may be available. With access to top-tier facilities and a flourishing sector that offers a wealth of possibilities for real-world experience and jobs, Dubai also provides a dynamic atmosphere for pursuing tourism and hospitality.

Eligibility Criteria to pursue Tourism and Hospitality in Dubai

The eligibility criteria to pursue Tourism and Hospitality in Dubai are as under:

  • The student needs to be atleast 17 years of age.
  • He/she should have completed a high school diploma or comparable.
  • The student should have a good academic performance and submit transcripts from all education institutions previously attended.
  • The student needs to fulfill the minimum GPA prerequisite (differs depending on the institution, usually around 2.0-2.5 on a 4.0 scale).
  • Scores of standardized tests viz., the SAT or comparable may be required by certain universities.
  • Non-native English speakers need to prove their fluency in English through appearing for English language proficiency tests viz., the IELTS or TOEFL.
  • Pertinent work experience or previous education in associated fields could be advantageous.
  • Submission of a written statement highlighting the student’s professional objectives and rationale behind pursuing the course.
  • Generally 2 recommendation letters from instructors or employers.
  • The student needs to provide proof of adequate funds to pay for tuition and living expenses.
  • Evidence of health insurance coverage for the entire study duration.
  • Current passport and student visa for overseas students.

FAQs:

  1. For students pursuing tourism and hospitality in Dubai, are there any scholarships or financial aid opportunities available?

    Yes, students studying tourism and hospitality in Dubai have access to various scholarships viz.,

    • Institutional Scholarships: Scholarships based on merit or need are offered by several universities.
    • Government Scholarships: Sometimes provided by the UAE government or local authorities.
    • Private Scholarships: Available via industry groups or private organizations.
  2. How can overseas students locate internships or work experience in Dubai?

    Overseas students can locate internships or work experience in Dubai via.,

    • University Placement Services: Internship placement services are offered by a number of institutions.
    • Online Job Portals: Opportunities for internships and jobs are listed on websites viz., Bayt, Indeed, and LinkedIn.
    • Networking Events: Attending career fairs and industry events.
    • Direct Applications: Applying to tour operators, hotels, and event management firms directly.
