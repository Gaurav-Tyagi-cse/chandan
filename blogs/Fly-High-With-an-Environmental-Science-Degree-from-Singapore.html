Fly High With an Environmental Science Degree from Singapore

Owing to its outstanding educational system, central location in Asia, and multicultural setting, Singapore is an alluring study abroad destination for overseas students that provides varied academic and cultural encounters. Moreover, its robust economy and worldwide connectivity offer a wealth of possibilities for internships and future employment opportunities.

Concentrating on the study of ecosystems, sustainable development, and pollution control, Environmental Science programs in Singapore deal with both local as well as worldwide environmental concerns. Undergraduate programs generally take 3-4 years to complete, whereas, postgraduate programs last 1-2 years.

Why study Environmental Science in Singapore?

There are several reasons for students to think about studying Environmental Science in Singapore:

  • Academic Prowess: Acquire top-notch instruction from elite universities with specialized programs in environmental science.
  • State-of-the-art Facilities: Students who study in Singapore gain access to cutting-edge research facilities and technology in environmental science.
  • Central Location: Owing to its status as a global centre, Singapore provides distinct possibilities to study environmental concerns pertinent to urban and tropical climates.
  • Varied Ecosystems: Students pursuing Environmental Science have the chance to learn about a range of ecosystems from tropical rainforests to urban environments.
  • Government Projects: Robust national initiatives and policies that emphasize green technology and sustainability.
  • Worldwide Viewpoint: Interact wih experts from around the world and engage in international environmental discussions.
  • Employment Prospects: There is an increasing demand for environmental specialists across a range of industries, including NGOs, the goverment, and private sector.

Best Environmental Science specializations to pursue in Singapore

The best Environmental Science specializations for students to pursue in Singapore are as listed below:

  • Environmental Engineering
  • Urban Environmental Management
  • Renewable Energy Technologies

Leading universities in Singapore to pursue Environmental Science

The prominent universities in Singapore to pursue Environmental Science are:

  • National University of Singapore (NUS)
  • Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD)
  • Nanyang Technological University (NTU)
  • Singapore Management University (SMU)

What’s the cost of pursuing Environmental Science in Singapore?

Based on the institution and the program, the cost of pursuing Environmental Science in Singapore differs. International students pay between SGD 20,000-SGD 40,000 in tuition annually at public universities viz., the National University of Singapore (NUS). On the contrary, local students pay considerably lower tuition fees, usually between SGD 8,000 and SGD 12,000 per year. Varied fee structures, frequently higher than public universities may apply to private institutions or specialized programs. Moreover, students should also set aside money for living costs, which might cost an additional SGD 10,000-SGD 20,000 annually.

Eligibility Criteria to pursue Environmental Science in Singapore

Mentioned below are the eligibility criteria to pursue Environmental Science in Singapore:

  • The student needs to hold a pertinent undergraduate degree in Environmental Science, Chemistry, Biology, or an associated field.
  • He/she needs to have a strong academic record, usually with a minimum GPA or comparable.
  • Entrance exams or standardized tests viz., SAT or ACT may be required by certain institutions for undergraduate programs. For postgraduate programs, GRE tests scores may be required.
  • Non-native English speakers need to prove their fluency in English through appearing for English language proficiency tests viz., the IELTS or TOEFL and submit scores of the same.
  • Pertinent work experience or internships may be required particularly for postgraduate programs. Participating in extra-curricular activities pertaining to environmental science can be advantageous.
  • Pertinent research experience may be advantageous.
  • Submission of a well-written statement of purpose highlighting the student’s interest and objectives in Environmental Science.
  • Compelling recommendation letters from instructors or employers.
  • Overseas students need to meet student visa prerequisites.
  • Students need to provide evidence of sufficient finances to pay for their studies in Singapore.
  • Each university may have its own set of criteria and requirements. For specific prerequisites and application processes, visit the websites of each university.

FAQs:

  1. Are overseas students eligible to apply?

    Yes, overseas students are welcome by the majority of universities in Singapore who also provide assistance to help them acclimatise to living and studying in Singapore.

  2. Are there any possibilities for scholarships or monetary assistance available?

    There are numerous possibilities for scholarships or monetary assistance that are available. They are:

    • University-Specific Scholarships: Scholarships based on merit or need are offered by a number of universities.
    • Government Scholarships: A variety of scholarships for both local as well as overseas students are offered by the government in Singapore.
    • Private Sector and NGO Grants: For students pursuing this field, scholarships or funding are provided by a few environmental NGOs and businesses.
