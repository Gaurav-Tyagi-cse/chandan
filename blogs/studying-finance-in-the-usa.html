Know All About Studying Finances in the USA

Studying finance in the US offers a wide range of opportunities for students from all over the world. The USA is a prime destination for advancing careers by providing in-hand information about different principles and concepts. There are diverse specialisations available that are offered at some of the best institutions in the USA that help students build a career in the field of finance.

Why does the USA stand out for its financial programmes?


Well-reputed institutions:

The USA is home to premier institutions that provide better opportunities and programmes in finance and related fields. The universities are known for being top-notch educational institutions that provide a good career in finance.

Many specialisations:

From bachelor's to master's programmes, there are a wide range of specialisations offered by finance universities in the USA. Students will get a chance to learn from various specialisations and varied concepts like commerce, finance, operations, marketing, and much more.

Good job opportunities:

After completing a programme, graduates become eligible for a big career not only in the USA but also in many other countries. As the USA is home to many businesses, getting a job here is feasible.

Best universities in USA for finance

Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) : Founded in 1861, MIT is one of the finest institutions for finance in the USA. The university offers a profitable finance programme for all its students. MIT has a wide range of specialisations at the bachelor's, master's, and doctoral levels. The various topics included in the curriculum are business law, taxation, finance management, industrial business, and much more in the finance courses.

Columbia University : Columbia University is one of the finest private universities in the USA for finance. The university attracts international students from all over the world. There are varied finance programmes that help develop a good understanding of the subjects. Also, there are many programmes available at postgraduate and undergraduate levels that provide education in finance, accounting, business management, taxation laws, business laws, indirect taxes, administration, and a lot more.

UC Berkely : Among the most prominent schools in the world, the University of California Berkeley's Haas School of Business provides students from all over the world with the top finance programmes. Bachelor's, master's, and doctorate degrees in finance are available to students. The Haas School of Business offers a variety of programmes, including the Bachelor of Management and Entrepreneurship. In addition, students can select from weekend and evening finance classes to receive the finest possible instruction. UC Berkley offers an intensive one-year programme called the Master of Financial Engineering. Applied finance projects and internship programmes are also offered to students.

University of Texas (Austin) : The University of Texas at Austin is a public university that was established in 1883 and offers some of the best STEM and finance programmes in the state. This place gives students the chance to learn about financial subjects including accounting, financial management, business administration, taxation, economics, and much more from first-hand experience. The Finance programme has included industry-related projects and internships to help students better grasp their roles in the professional world beyond the academic year. Therefore, it is among the top universities for choosing a financial curriculum. Numerous finance courses are available at this university, ranging from undergraduate to graduate degrees.

University of Illinois (Chicago) : Studying finance at the University of Chicago has numerous benefits, such as having in-depth knowledge and gaining experience with concepts like financial accounting, business law, tax law, business analytics, business administration, behavioural science, and a lot more. Additionally, the university offers internships and work opportunities so that students can gain first-hand experience in learning.

Specialisations for finance programmes

The finance programmes in the USA are known for their huge demand because of various specialisations. The different specialisations include:

  • Corporate Finance
  • Investment Management
  • Financial Analysis
  • Financial Engineering
  • Risk Management
  • Real estate finance
  • International Finance
  • Financial Technology (Fintech)
  • Sustainable Finance
  • Behavioural Finance

What are the eligibility criteria for finance programmes?

  1. A high school certificate or equivalent, or a bachelor's degree or equivalent, which is always applicable for students with minimum marks set up by the university of application.
  2. English language proficiency tests such as IELTS and TOEFL, along with additional entrance tests such as the GRE or GMAT, depending on the institution.
  3. Students would require prior work experience of at least 2 years.

Documents required for finance courses in the USA:

  1. Academic transcripts
  2. Statement of Purpose
  3. Valid passport
  4. Portfolio
  5. Current passport-sixed photographs
  6. ELP test scores
  7. Letter of Recommendation
  8. Proof of financial support
