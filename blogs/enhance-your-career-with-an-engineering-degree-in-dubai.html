Enhance Your Career With an Engineering Degree in Dubai

Owing to its first-rate educational institutions, varied multicultural setting, and central location that provides students with distinct employment opportunities in a rapidly expanding global centre, Dubai is a popular study abroad destination. Students from all over the world are drawn to the city because it offers state-of-the-art academic facilities along with a dynamic lifestyle and a thriving labor market.

With an emphasis on cutting-edge technology, contemporary infrastructure, and innovation fueled by the region’s accelerated expansion and educational spending, in Dubai, undegraduate i.e. Bachelor’s degrees generally take 4 years to complete and postgraduate i.e. Master’s degree typically span 1-2 years. Based on the research topic and university prerequisites, PhD programs typically take 3-4 years.

Why study Engineering in Dubai?

Studying Engineering in Dubai is a popular choice for the following reasons:

  • Top-Tier Instruction: Dubai is home to prestigious universities with excellent engineering programs and accreditation.
  • Language: English being the medium of instruction, makes it easier for overseas students to study.
  • State-of-the-art Infrastructure: State-of-the-art facilities and innovative technology in education and research.
  • Safety and Standard of Living: Secure city with an excellent quality of life, contemporary amenities, and a thriving lifestyle.
  • Varied Setting: Students who study in Dubai have the chance to study in a cosmopolitan city that exposes him/her to diverse cultures and worldwide viewpoints.
  • Scholarships and Monetary Assistance: Overseas students have access to scholarships and financial possibilities.
  • Innovation and Development: Concentrate on technological development and smart city projects.
  • Central Location: Central centre that links East and West, offering worldwide networking possibilities.
  • Economic Possibilities: Dubai boasts a dynamic economy with several employment possibilities in engineering industries.
  • Collaborations with Industry: Partnerships with prominent firms and industries for internships and real-world experience.

What’s the cost of pursuing Engineering in Dubai?

Based on the university and the particular program, the cost of pursuing an engineering degree in Dubai differs. Typically, for undergraduate engineering programs, the tuition fees range from AED 50,000-AED 80,000 annually. Postgraduate programs on the contrary, cost between AED 55,000-AED 120,000 in tuition annually. Supplementary expenditures viz., living costs, textbooks, and housing, which can considerably increase the total cost, are not entailed in these costs.

Apart from tuition, students should set aside money for housing, which depending on the location and kind of lodging can differ significantly costing between AED 20,000-AED 40,000 annually for shared apartments to substantially higher for private housing. Supplementary expenses include food, transit, and other individual expenditures, usually costing between AED 15,000-AED 25,000 annually. All in all, in Dubai, the overall expense for an engineering student, including tuition and living expenditures, is estimated to be between AED 85,000-AED 150,000.

Eligibility Criteria to pursue Engineering in Dubai

The eligibility criteria to pursue Engineering in Dubai are:

  • The student needs to complete secondary education (high school) with an emphasis on mathematics and science.
  • He/she needs to submit academic transcripts from all education institutions previously attended.
  • The student should fulfill the minimum GPA prerequisite, usually around 2.5-3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • He/she may need to submit scores of standardized tests viz., the SAT, ACT, or comparable.
  • Non-native English speakers need to prove their fluency in English through appearing for English language proficiency tests viz., the IELTS or TOEFL.
  • Submission of a personal statement.
  • Recommendation letters.
  • An interview or entrance exam may be demanded by certain institutions.
  • The student needs to provide evidence of his/her ability to pay for tuition and living costs.
  • Copy of passport.
  • To study in Dubai, the student needs to hold a valid student visa.
  • Submission of a filled-out application form for the selected institution.
