All About Pursuing Architecture in Dubai

A dynamic, cosmopolitan setting with first-rate universities, contemporary infrastructure, and ample possibilities for career development and networking is offered by Dubai, thereby, making it an alluring study abroad destination for overseas students. Moreover, its central location and rich cultural legacy offer a distinctive and rewarding study abroad experience.

The architecture in Dubai is well-known for its cutting-edge and contemporary designs, featuring famous landmarks viz., the Palm Jumeirah and the Burj Khalifa, that combine modernistic design with traditional elements. In Dubai, undergraduate degrees in architecture generally take 3-5 years to complete, while postgraduate degrees span 1-2 years.

Why study Architecture in Dubai?

One should think about studying architecture in Dubai for the following reasons:

  • Central Location: Gateway to developing markets in the Middle East and Africa.
  • Vibrant Setting: Continuous growth and developing skyline, providing new challenges and inspiration.
  • Amalgamation of Cultures: Assimilation of traditional Islamic and Contemporary Western architectural designs.
  • Famous Landmarks: Experience in structuring and studying iconic structures viz., the Burj Khalifa.
  • Cutting-edge Technology: Access to the most recent resources and technologies in architecture and construction.
  • Cutting-edge Architecture: Exposure to state-of-the-art designs and forward-thinking initiatives.
  • Worldwide Viewpoint: Engaging with global architects and varied design principles.
  • Economic Centre: Possibilities for internships and jobs in a vibrant construction market.

Prominent universities in Dubai to pursue Architecture

The prominent universities in Dubai to pursue Architecture are as under:

  • Heriot-Watt University Dubai
  • University of Sharjah
  • Al Ghurair University
  • Dubai Institute of Design and Innovation (DIDI)
  • American University in Dubai (AUD)

What’s the cost of pursuing Architecture in Dubai?

An architectural degree in Dubai usually costs between AED 60,000-AED 100,000 annually. Tuition fees which can differ based on the institution and length of the program, are entailed in this. Supplementary expenditures may include supplies, books, and other student fees, which could increase the overall annual cost by several thousand dirhams. Institutions viz., the University of Sharjah and the American University in Dubai are renowned for their architecture programs and can offer precise fee structures.

Eligibility Criteria to pursue Architecture in Dubai

Mentioned below are the eligibility criteria to pursue Architecture in Dubai:

  • The student needs to have completed high school i.e. 12th grade with an emphasis on mathematics and science. For Master’s degrees, the student needs to hold a Bachelor’s degree in Architecture or an associated field from an accredited university.
  • He/she should fulfill the minimum GPA prerequisite of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • The student needs to submit academic transcripts from all education institutions previously attended.
  • Scores of standardized tests viz., the SAT or ACT may be required by certain institutions.
  • Non-native English speakers need to prove their fluency in English through appearing for English language proficiency tests viz., the IELTS or TOEFL.
  • Pertinent work experience or internships may be favored or required by certain programs.
  • The candidate needs to submit an essay or personal statement outlining his/her motivation, objectives, and interest in architecture.
  • Letters of recommendation.
  • A portfolio highlighting his/her prior work and design abilities needs to be submitted by the student.
  • The student needs to clear an entrance exam or interview, as required by certain institutions.
  • He/she needs to provide evidence of adequate finances to pay for tuition and living costs.
  • Submission of a filled-out application form for the selected architecture program.
  • To study in Dubai, the student needs to hold a valid student visa.
  • Overseas students need to comply with visa and residency prerequisites.

FAQs:

  1. Do architecture students in Dubai have access to scholarships?

    Scholarships based on financial need, academic performance, or other criteria are offered by several universities. In addition, grants or scholarships may be provided by certain private organizations and government agencies.

  2. When selecting an architecture university in Dubai, what factors should students consider?

    Variables viz., the accreditation, course syllabus, qualifications of the faculty members, internship possibilities, facilities, and overall standing should be taken into account by students. Visiting the campus, and if feasible, communicating with alumni or students who are presently studying there is also beneficial.
