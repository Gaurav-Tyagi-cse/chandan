Fly Towards Your Dreams By Studying Aviation in Dubai

Dubai is considered to be the global hub for business, tourism, and luxury. The city offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition by providing a welcoming environment for all people while remaining true to its core values. Its vibrant cosmopolitan culture, world-class shopping, and uniquely blended culinary scenes attract visitors from around the globe.

Dubai’s strategic location as the bridge between the East and the West is an important reason as to why the Dubai international airport is the second busiest airport in the world by foot traffic. Dubai, because of this, is home to a thriving aerospace industry with world-class educational institutions in the aviation space. With a growing network of airlines and aviation companies, students gain access to cutting-edge technology and practical experience. Programs often include hands-on training at international airports and opportunities to network with industry professionals from all over the world.

Why Study Aviation in Dubai?

  • As a leading centre for business and tourism Dubai acts as a hotspot for brushing shoulders with the best-of-the-best of professionals in every industry in the world.
  • Dubai has the second busiest airport in the world which leads to an abundance of opportunity for growing your career and facilitating interaction with global talent in the aviation space.
  • The aviation institutions often collaborate with top-tier airlines and aerospace companies based in Dubai.
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities and simulation centres for hands-on training.
  • Opportunities to participate in international aviation events and conferences.

Top Universities in Dubai to Pursue Aviation

Here are some of the top universities in Dubai to pursue aviation in and the specializations that they offer:

  1. Emirates Aviation University: Known for its specialized aviation programs, including bachelor's and master's degrees in aviation management, aerospace engineering, and pilot training.
  2. Dubai Aviation College: Offers a range of aviation-focused programs, including diplomas and degrees in aviation management, flight operations, and aircraft maintenance.
  3. University of Sharjah: Located near Dubai, it offers aerospace engineering programs with a strong focus on aviation technology and research.
  4. British University in Dubai: Provides graduate programs in aviation management and logistics.
  5. Al Ghurair University: Offers programs in aviation management and aviation engineering.

What’s the Cost of Pursuing Aviation in Dubai?

Studying aviation in Dubai consists of a wide range of programs, including diplomas, bachelor's degrees, and master's degrees in fields like aviation management, aerospace engineering, and flight training. The cost of pursuing aviation studies can thus vary significantly but usually it within the range of AED 40,000 to AED 100,000 per year. Living expenses in Dubai is dependant upon the lifestyle, accommodation, and personal preferences. On average, the living expenses which includes rent for a shared apartment utilities, food, transportation, and other personal expenses can cost somewhere between AED 2,500 and AED 5,000 per month.

Job Opportunities after Studying Aviation in Dubai

Studying aviation in Dubai can open a range of career opportunities, both within the UAE and internationally. Here are some potential career paths you may consider after completing your education in aviation in Dubai:

  1. Pilot: If you pursue flight training and obtain the necessary certifications, you could become a commercial or private pilot.
  2. Aircraft Maintenance Engineer: Specializing in maintaining and repairing aircraft, this role is crucial in ensuring safety and efficiency in flight operations.
  3. Air Traffic Controller: Managing the safe and orderly flow of air traffic, this role requires specialized training and certification.
  4. Aviation Management: This includes roles in airport management, airline operations, and aviation consultancy.
  5. Aerospace Engineering: Involves designing and developing aircraft and spacecraft. Although more specialized, Dubai’s growing aerospace sector offers opportunities with companies like Airbus and Boeing, which have regional operations.
  6. Flight Attendant: Working as a flight attendant involves ensuring passenger safety and comfort.
  7. Aviation Safety Inspector: Ensuring compliance with safety regulations and standards, this role typically requires experience and certification in aviation safety.

FAQs:

  1. Are internships or practical training included in aviation programs?

    Many aviation programs in Dubai include internships or practical training as part of the curriculum. For example pilot training programs have air plane simulators that simulate the real experience of flying a plane. In terms of internships, a lot of institutions work in close ties with the industry itself which gives students an opportunity to jump straight into a career in the aviation industry after graduation.

  2. What are the career prospects after studying aviation in Dubai?

    Graduates can pursue careers in various sectors, including airline operations, aircraft maintenance, aviation management, and air traffic control. Dubai’s status as a major aviation hub will ensure that there is no shortage of suitable jobs for someone who wants to work in the field.
