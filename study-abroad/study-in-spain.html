Why Study in Spain?

  • Highly ranked institutions

    Spain boasts several highly ranked universities, including the University of Barcelona and IE University, known for their academic excellence and innovative research.

  • Diverse Academic Programs

    Spanish universities offer a wide range of academic programs, right from the Arts and Humanities to Science, Technology, and Business. Students can select from a wide range of programs as per their interests and goals.

  • Affordable

    Compared to the USA and other European countries, the cost of living in Spain is lower, thus making it an affordable destination for international students.

  • Excellent faculty

    Spanish universities have professors and researchers who are experts in their fields. The faculty does not only have experience in academia but also has a practical approach in their teaching.

  • Good job opportunities

    Spain has a strong economy and a huge demand for skilled candidates in fields like Business Management, Marketing, and Communication. Studying in Spain gives ample opportunities for internships and job opportunities after graduating.

  • Study in English

    Universities in Spain offer English-taught and bilingual study programs alongside Spanish, thus accommodating international students. These options allow students who have not mastered Spanish to pursue education in their preferred language.

Scholarships in Spain

Spain scholarships for Indians students usually cover tuition, living, and sometimes travel expenses. Offered by the government, universities, and organizations, these scholarships cater to various backgrounds and disciplines. Notable options include the Erasmus+ and Fulbright programs, as well as Spain's own "Becas" initiatives. Many universities also offer scholarships based on merit or need. Prospective students should research and apply for relevant scholarships early to enhance their chances of funding their studies in Spain.

FAQs

  • Can I study and work with my student visa?

    Current legislation allows students to work while in possession of a student visa and resident permit (NIE estudiante) as long as they do not work full-time and the work does not interfere with their studies. You are allowed to work 30 hours per week when you decide to study abroad in Spain.

  • Do I need to have some Spanish language skills to attend a programme?

    Knowing Spanish especially for studying Master’s in Spain for Indian students is not an issue since courses are available in 100% English.

  • What are the documents required to study in Spain for Indian students?

    Documents such as a valid passport, two passport-sized photographs, a health insurance form, a criminal background check form, evidence of sufficient funds for a stay, No-objection certificate from school or university, A letter of acceptance from Spain’s university or school and two Schengen application forms duly filled out and signed

  • Is Spain a very expensive country to study?

    Spain is an affordable country. The cost to study abroad in Spain will depend on the location, type of university, the program and the university, but on average it will cost you around EUR 18,000 per year.

  • What is the cost of studying a Master degree in Spain?

    The tuition fees for studying a Master degree in Spain is approximately between EUR 4,000 to 15,000 per year.
