FAQs

  • What are the top universities in the USA for international students?

    The USA is home to the highest number of top ranked universities in the world making it an ideal study abroad destination for international students. Some of the top universities in the USA include Harvard University, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), Stanford University, University of California, Berkeley, and Princeton University.

  • Can I get financial assistance to study in the USA?

    Yes, the cost of studying in USA can be significantly reduced with the help of financial assistance if they match the eligibility criteria, such as good test scores, excellent academic records, and other criteria set by the universities.

  • Should I apply only to the top ten universities?

    The acceptance rates of the top ten universities are 1–4%. It is advised to apply to other universities as well. As one of the top USA education consultants, we will help ensure that you apply to the best universities providing the highest quality education.

  • Do universities in USA accept only TOEFL or can a student also take IELTS?

    While TOEFL is generally more commonly accepted by universities in the USA, many universities also accept IELTS as proof of English language proficiency.

  • I have completed 15 years of education in India. Can I get admission to an MBA program directly in the USA?

    Yes, many universities do accept 15 years of education with or without work experiences directly for an MBA. However, few do require 16 years of education.
